As we all know the Rail transport is the backbone of the tranportaion almost all Country. But do we ever know how does it work and what can it do and how many people has been involved in this. If we take example of India we will astonished that India has higher number of employees in Railways sector than any other Government organisation of India. According to Ministry of Railways(New Delhi) there are 1.23 million employees are currently working with Indian Railways. Indian Railways is one of the public facilities given by the Government and manages the fourth largest railways network in the world.
